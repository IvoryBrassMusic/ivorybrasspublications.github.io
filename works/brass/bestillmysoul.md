# Be Still, My Soul

Instrumentation: 5 Horns
Duration: 2 minutes, 25 seconds
Difficulty: Early Advanced

---

<b>Description</b>
*Be Still, My Soul* is a well-known hymn based on Jean Sibelius's *Finlandia*. The words of this hymn are an encouragement to oneself to rest in the care of the Lord throughout life's various circumstances. Suitable for church performance or concert programming, this arrangement for horn quintet uses the rich and noble timbre of the horn to communicate this powerful message.
