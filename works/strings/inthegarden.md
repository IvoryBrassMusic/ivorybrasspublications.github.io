# In the Garden

Instrumentation: Violin and Piano  
Duration: 2 minutes, 55 seconds 
Difficuly: Late Intermediate

---

<b>Description</b>
*In the Garden* is a solo arrangement of C. Austin Miles's hymn *In the Garden*. First published in 1912, it describes the intimate fellowship a believer has with Jesus Christ. Suitable for church performance or recital programming, this arrangement uses rich harmonies to complement the poetic language of the hymn's lyrics.
